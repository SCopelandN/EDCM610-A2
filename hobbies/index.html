Polymer Clay

The one that keeps me creative!

This hobby allows me to stay creative. The creation possibilities are endless when working with it. Polymer clay is a type of modeling clay that is made from vinyl. I love polymer clay because it stays soft until you bake it in an oven. Unlike air dry clay, it won’t dry out so you can keep the leftovers to use for another time. It is versatile and you can blend it to create custom colors. You can also sculpt it, stamp it, emboss textures on it and even paint it. Polymer clay is used to make a variety of things but my favorite is earrings.
